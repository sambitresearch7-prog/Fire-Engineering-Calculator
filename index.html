Method Notes

Step 1 – Pre-filter: All 605 stations within the selected radius (straight-line Haversine) are identified from the built-in database.
Step 2 – Road routing: The closest 15 pre-filtered stations are routed via the OSRM public API (OpenStreetMap) to obtain driving distance (km) and estimated travel time.
Step 3 – Rank: Stations are ranked by driving distance and the top 5 are displayed with a route map.
